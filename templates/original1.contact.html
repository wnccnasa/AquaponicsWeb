🎓 Join the STEM Club

The WNCC STEM Club welcomes students interested in:

  • Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics
  • Hands-on projects and experiments
  • Environmental sustainability
  • Aquaponics and sustainable agriculture
  • Technology and monitoring systems
  • Career exploration in STEM fields

No experience required! We welcome students of all skill levels who are curious about STEM.
